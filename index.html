About Me

UI/UX Designer with Technical Background

Hello

My name is Rofaida, a 23-year-old engineer from Nasr City, Cairo, Egypt. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Architecture Engineering from the Egyptian Russian University in June 2022.

I am so passionate about learning new stuff. Since I started college, I haven't stopped learning about other fields besides architecture, including cosmology, psychology, and physiology, etc. I have also taught myself many skills such as programming and logo design. The skill that has helped me the most is time management and planning. I enjoy expressing myself through drawing and designing, and this website will help you get to know more about me.
